6.6 Deleting a Websheet Application

Delete a Websheet on the Websheet Properties page.

To delete a Websheet application:

  1. On the Workspace home page, click the App Builder icon.
    The App Builder home page appears.
  2. Select the Websheet application.
    The Websheet Properties page appears.
  3. Click Delete.
